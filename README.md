## Name of the Project: Keen Keeper

## Description: 
Keen Keeper is a modern relationship and communication management platform designed to help users stay closely connected with the important people in their lives. The project focuses on organizing and maintaining meaningful relationships by allowing users to categorize their close contacts based on personal and professional connections such as family members, friends, colleagues, and work-related people. Through the platform, users can easily communicate using text messaging, voice calls, and video calls, creating a complete communication experience in one place. Keen Keeper aims to simplify social connection management by providing a clean, user-friendly, and responsive interface where users can quickly access their favorite or most important contacts. The main goal of the project is to strengthen everyday human connections and ensure that valuable relationships remain active, organized, and easy to maintain in today’s busy digital lifestyle.

## Technologies Used:
- React.js
- Tailwind CSS, DaisyUI
- JavaScript (ES6+)
- React-Toastify (NPM Package)
- JSON (for product data)

## Features:
1. Users can organize and manage important people in their lives by categorizing contacts into groups such as family, friends, and work connections for easier communication and relationship management.

2. The platform supports multiple communication methods including text messaging, voice calls, and video calls, allowing users to stay connected with close people anytime and from anywhere.

3. Keen Keeper provides a clean, responsive, and user-friendly interface that works smoothly across mobile, tablet, and desktop devices, ensuring an accessible experience on all screen sizes.
